Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-29

This Privacy Policy describes how Vaslix Games (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information in the mobile applications we publish, including Gridlix (package com.vaslix.games.gridlix) and any future titles.

1. Information We Collect

Our apps do not require account creation and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly. The third-party services described below may collect technical and advertising-related information automatically, including:

  • Device advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate geographic location derived from it
  • Device model, operating system version, language, and time zone
  • App version, session timestamps, and crash diagnostics
  • Aggregated, non-personally-identifying usage events

3. Legal Basis and Consent (GDPR / EEA, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, the legal basis for advertising-related processing is your consent, collected via an IAB Transparency & Consent Framework (TCF) dialog presented when you first launch the app. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time from within the app.

4. CCPA / US State Privacy Laws

If you are a California resident (or in another applicable US state), you have the right to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of your personal information for behavioral advertising. The in-app consent dialog provides this opt-out where required.

5. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

Our apps are not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain personal information directly. The advertising partners listed above retain data per their own policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Because we do not directly hold this data, requests should be directed to the advertising partner concerned.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above indicates when the most recent change was made.
